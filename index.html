TAREQ M. MORETAZA HASAN

Managing Director

After completing his Honours and Masters in Political Science from the University of Dhaka, Tareque started his journalism career with the country’s leading Bangla daily Prothom Alo as a city correspondent. He is the Monajat Uddin Memorial Investigative Award winner journalist.

Mr. Tareque joined Impact PR as a senior consultant in June 2011 and is playing role as account manager for Robi Axiata Ltd, edotco, LG Bangladesh, Water Aid.
